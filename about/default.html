About Company

Goods transport in India has become more specialized than ever before. With technology changing the way business is done, we at Sitaram Transport Co. too have introduced new concepts for taking on the new challenges and demands, and meeting the expectations of our customers.

With the economy opening up, and increasing foreign investments, We are all geared up to meet the new challenges.

Foundation

Sitaram Transport Co. had a humble beginning in the year of 2005 with a single truck under the able leadership of Mr. Manji Ahir.

Since then company have transformed itself as one of the leading business houses in Kandla Complex, and that too in such a short span of time. Presently being reputed as a prominent logistics service provider, we are having hundreds of fleets and network spread across all over india.

Assets

Our Company is governed on the basis of sound Mission, Vision and Quality Policy. We consider it as the biggest asset we have to lead this epic industry.

One of the company’s greatest strengths today is its infrastructure and it has extensively invested in them. We are equipped with latest material handling equipments for seamless operations.
We are 100% computerized and networked through our customized ERP program, ‘FreightMan’ allowing access to information & to trace and track your consignments in a matter of seconds.

The company’s well-established reputation is built upon a wealth of experience in all the major markets and industries, with specialization in the fast-moving and increasingly prominent Global market. Here we have excelled on the strength of our vast knowledge base of this ever changing market area.
